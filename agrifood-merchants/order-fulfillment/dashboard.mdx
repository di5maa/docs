## Dashboard Layout

The dashboard is divided into three main areas to help you process orders efficiently:

1. **Header & Quick Stats:** Immediate visibility on order status and totals.
2. **Left Column (Details):** Customer notes, item lists, payment breakdowns, and fulfillment timeline.
3. **Right Column (Actions):** Management tools, customer contact details, and driver information.

## 1. Quick Stats & Header

At the top of the panel, you will find high-level metrics for the specific order.

- **Order ID & Status:** Unique reference number (e.g., `#12345`) and the current status pill (e.g., `PENDING`, `COMPLETED`).
- **Timeline:** Shows when the order was placed relative to now.
- **Total Value:** The final order total including all fees and tips.
- **Fulfillment Type:** Indicates if the order is for **Delivery** or **Pickup**.
- **Distance/Time:** \* _For Delivery:_ Shows the distance to the customer (e.g., "3.5 km").
  - _For Pickup:_ Shows how long ago the order was placed (e.g., "15 min ago").

### Updating Order Status

Use the **Management** card in the right column to progress the order:

1. Click **Update Status**.
2. Select the new status (e.g., "Ready for Pickup").
3. The timeline will update automatically to reflect the change.

### The Picklist

Click the **Picklist** button to open a dedicated view for picking items from your inventory. This is useful for large grocery or retail orders where checking off items ensures accuracy.

## 3. Order Details

### Customer Notes

<Info>
   **Attention Required:** If the customer has left a note (e.g., "Leave at front door", "Allergies"), it will appear in a prominent yellow box at the top of the details column. Always review this first. 
</Info>

### Logistics Info

For delivery orders, a Logistics card displays:

- **Scheduled Time:** The expected delivery or pickup window.
- **Address:** The delivery destination.
- **Edit Date:** You can modify the expected delivery date if delays occur by clicking the **"Edit"** button.

### Ordered Items

A list of all items in the order.

- **Tap an item** to view detailed options (e.g., selected toppings, variants, or specific unit measurements).
- **Images:** Thumbnail images help quick visual identification.

### Payment Summary

A complete breakdown of the financial transaction:

- Subtotal
- Delivery Fees (or "Free")
- Driver Tips
- Taxes & Platform Fees
- **Discounts:** Promotional discounts are highlighted in green.

## Common Questions

**Why don't I see the "Update Status" or "Picklist" buttons?**

These features are hidden for **Restaurant** accounts because they typically use a separate Kitchen Display System (KDS) flow. If you are a retail or grocery vendor and cannot see these buttons, please contact support to verify your account type.

**Can I print invoices for thermal printers?**

Yes, the **Print Invoice** feature generates a responsive PDF that scales well for both standard office printers and smaller receipt (thermal) printers.
