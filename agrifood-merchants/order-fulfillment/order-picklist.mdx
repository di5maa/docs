## Overview

Instead of printing paper lists, staff can use the Picklist to:

- Track packing progress in real-time.
- Scan item barcodes to verify products.
- Organize items by aisle or category.

## Interface Guide

### 1. Progress Header

At the top of the screen, you will see the **Order Number** and a **Progress Circle**.

- **Visual Tracking:** The circle fills up as you mark items as "Picked".
- **Counter:** Shows exactly how many items are done vs. total (e.g., `12/45`).

## 3. Picking Items

The list is automatically organized by **Category** (e.g., Produce, Dairy, General) to help you navigate your store efficiently.

### Marking Items

- **Tap an Item:** Tap anywhere on the item card to mark it as **Done**.
- **Visual Cues:**
  - **Unpicked:** White background, clear text.
  - **Picked:** Green background, green border, and completed text.

### Item Details & Variations

Some items may have specific variations (e.g., "Size: Large" or "Flavor: Strawberry").

- **Information:** Visual tags show preparation time (e.g., "1d Prep") and unit measurements (e.g., "500g").
- **View Options:** If an item has complex options, **press and hold** (or tap details) to see a popup list of selected modifiers and their prices.

<Warning>
  **Attention Required:** Always check the quantity box on the right side of the item card. It displays the required amount in large, bold text (e.g., **5** Qty).
</Warning>

## 4. Troubleshooting

| Issue                  | Solution                                                                                                     |
| :--------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Item Not Found**     | Ensure the product code (SKU) in your inventory matches the physical barcode. Try searching by name instead. |
| **Scanner Dark**       | Tap the **Flashlight** icon in the bottom center of the scanner view.                                        |
| **Wrong Item Scanned** | The app will vibrate and show a "Not Found" message to prevent packing mistakes.                             |
