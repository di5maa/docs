## 1. General Information

This is the default view when you open settings. It controls the core details of your business.

### Basic Profile

- **Store Name:** The official name displayed on your storefront.
- **Tagline:** A short, catchy phrase appearing under your name (e.g., "Fresh from the farm").
- **Description:** A full summary of your business history and values.
- **Cuisine/Business Type:** Select the category that best fits you (e.g., Bakery, Butcher, Cafe) to help with search filtering.

### Contact Info

- **Email & Phone:** Public contact details for customers.
- **Website:** A link to your external website (if you have one).

### Sales Model

Toggle the ways you interact with customers. You can enable multiple options:

- **Retail:** Selling individual items to consumers.
- **Wholesale:** Selling in bulk to other businesses.
- **Takeout:** Customers pick up orders.
- **Dine-in:** (Restaurant only) Customers eat at your location.

### Business Attributes

Enable these badges to highlight your business identity on the marketplace:

- **Women-owned**
- **Indigenous-owned**
- **Cultural Heritage** (Ethnic Store)

## 2. Address & Location

Ensure customers and delivery drivers can find you.

- **Search Address:** Start typing your address in the search bar. Select the correct match from the dropdown list.
- **Confirmation:** Once selected, the system updates your "Current address" coordinates. This is used to calculate delivery fees and show your store on the customer map.

## 3. Appearance (Branding)

Customize the visual look of your store page.

- **Logo:** The circular icon representing your brand.
  - _Best size:_ Square (1:1 ratio).
- **Cover Photo:** The large banner image behind your logo.
  - _Best size:_ Wide landscape (16:9 ratio).

> **Note:** Click the "Upload" button to choose a file from your device. Click "Reset" to remove the current image.

## 4. Store Growth

Local Pass uses its own search engine to display visitors the most relevant products and businesses. Improve your visibility in the app search bar using **Keywords**.

- **How it works:** Enter words related to your products (e.g., "Gluten Free", "Spicy", "Vegan", "Local").
- **Add Keyword:** Type the word and tap the **"+"** button.
- **Remove:** Tap the **"X"** on a keyword chip to remove it.

<Note>
  Learn how to improve ranking on Local Pass. 
</Note>

## 5. Social Media

Link your external social profiles so customers can follow you.

- **Supported Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, WeChat.
- **Input:** Paste the full URL to your profile or your username depending on the platform.

## 6. Awards & Certifications

Build trust by displaying your achievements.

### Certifications

When you upload certifications, a "Green Badge" becomes visible on your account and listing. The badge shows Locals that your business follows the highest standards and trustworthy. Upload official documents (PDF or Image) such as:

- Food Safety Certificates
- Organic Certification
- Halal/Kosher Certification

### Awards

Showcase recognition your business has received.

- **Add Award:** Enter the **Award Name** and the **Year** received. You can optionally upload a photo of the award or badge.

## 7. Support & Account
